# Profile Picture and CV Setup

## Adding Your Profile Picture

1. Save your profile picture as `images/profile.jpg` (or `.png`)
2. Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square aspect ratio)
3. The image will be automatically cropped to a circle with a glowing border

If no image is found, a placeholder with your initial "M" will be displayed.

## Adding Your CV

1. Save your CV as `cv/Marco_Bertolino_CV.pdf`
2. The download button will appear automatically in the hero section
